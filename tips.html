Here are some tips:

  • Try to have a routine.

  • It's is important to exercise and do fun activities at least once a day, if possible.

  • Don't let your child spend too much time watching tv or playing with eletronics.

  • Eletronics may help to distrac children, even help them to learn. But outside activities are as important as the ones they do in school or at home with parents.

  • Make some food that they can help to prepare and learn something new.

  • It's very important for the development contact with different textures, smells and color.

  • Read books and watch some movie together.

  • Hug your children and say good things to them. That helps to build confidence and creativity.
